Persona

Name & Age:

Sam Hughes, 53

Profession:

Self-employed electrician works as a contractor and has his own business.

Technology:

  • Sam owns an HP Envy 2-in-1 Notebook that runs on the latest Windows 11 operating system.
  • He considers himself quite comfortable with new technology and on the computer as he is constantly doing tutorials on Youtube.
  • Sam uses both Firefox and Chrome as his preferred browsers.
  • His wife has an Apple iPad but he does not use hers. However, he uses his brand new iPhone 13 Pro a lot which runs the latest IOS 15 operating system.
  • Sam follows tech news on multiple channels (social media as well as regular tv news channels). He likes to stay up to date and also has some shares in big tech companies.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • Sam is very interested in technology and business news but he also loves being in nature. When his kids were small he used to take them on many hikes and camping trips.
  • Him and his wife Sue still enjoy the outdoors very much and enjoy going on hikes around New Mexico.
  • Both Sam and his wife have an interest in the history of New Mexico and are happy to volunteer on the weekends whenever there is help needed to preserve local landscapes. They help clean up and collect trash and plastic bottles because they enjoy intact landscapes when hiking.
  • Sam and his wife Sue also spend a lot of time gardening when springtime comes around as they genuinely enjoy being outside surrounded by nature.

Frustrations:

Sam disapproves of websites and images that take a long time to load.

He is frustrated by flashy images and GIFs and does not like websites that are difficult to navigate.

Navigation bars or menus are very important to him since he becomes easily irritated with bad layouts that give him a bad user experience.

He is frustrated whenever he sees littered landscapes on his hikes.

Goals:

  • He would like to contribute to preserving the natural landscapes of New Mexico for hikers like himself and the public in general.
  • Ideally, he would like to look at an archive that shows the landscapes of New Mexico and how they might have looked like long time ago.
  • He likes the idea of having a "time capsule" collection of New Mexico landscapes.
  • Sam is thinking about taking more pictures of landscapes on their hikes and maybe sharing them in the future.

SignUp Story:

As a user, I am looking for an easily accessible archive to browse images of New Mexico's landscapes.

Pre-Condition:

Sam does a search on Google for the Albuquerque Wildlife Federation's website.

Post-Condition:

Sam looks at the search results listed in Google.

Interaction Flow: Sam Hughes

  • a. Sam clicks on the link of the ABQ Wildlife Federation's website in the search results.
  • b. The browser takes Sam to the organization's homepage.
  • a. Sam looks at the navigation bar and clicks on the "Archive" tab.
  • b. A new page opens with a map and a list of New Mexico's landscapes.
  • a. Sam recognizes "Ceedro Creek" in the list and clicks on it.
  • b. The link takes him to a new page specific to the Ceedro Creek location.
  • a. Sam sees a carousel of images that were taken at the Ceedro Creek location. The images were taken at different dates. He clicks on the arrow in the carousel to move to the next image.
  • b. The arrow Sam clicked moves the current image to the left and shows him the next image.
  • a. Sam clicks the arrows to move back and forth between the images as he notices a form that says that users can upload their own pictures for Ceedro Creek here.
  • b. With each click on the arrows the gallery moves the images back and forth.
  • a. Sam is not yet ready to sign up for an account but will think about it. He closes the window.
