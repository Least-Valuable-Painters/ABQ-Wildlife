Persona

Name & Age:

Sam Hughes, 53

Profession:

Self-employed electrician works as a contractor and has his own business.

Technology:

  • Sam owns an HP Envy 2-in-1 Notebook that runs on the latest Windows 11 operating system.
  • He considers himself as quite comfortable with new technology and on the computer as he is constantly doing tutorials on Youtube.
  • Sam uses both Firefox and Chrome as his preferred browsers.
  • His wife has an Apple iPad but he does not use hers. However, he uses his brand new iPhone 13 Pro a lot which runs the latest IOS 15 operating system.
  • Sam follows tech news on multiple channels (social media as well as regular tv news channels). He likes to stay up to date and also has some shares in big tech companies.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

  • Sam is very interested in technology and business news but he also loves being in nature. When his kids were smaller he used to take them on many hikes and camping trips.
  • Him and his wife Sue still enjoy the outdoors very much and enjoy going on hikes around New Mexico.
  • Both Sam and his wife have an interest in the history of New Mexico as well and are happy to volunteer on the weekends whenever there is help needed to preserve local landscapes. They help clean up and collect trash and plastic bottles because they enjoy intact landscapes when hiking.
  • Sam and his wife Sue also spend a lot of time gardening when springtime comes around as they genuinely enjoy being outside surrounded by nature.

Frustrations:

Sam disapproves of websites and images that take a long time to load.

He is frustrated by flashy images and GIFs and does not like websites that are difficult to navigate.

Navigation bars or menus are very important to him since he becomes easily irritated with bad layouts that give him a bad user experience.

Goals:

  • Sam is looking for a way to publicly share the pictures he is taking on his hikes.
  • He would like to contribute to preserving the natural landscapes of New Mexico for hikers like himself and the public in general.
  • Ideally, he would be able to upload his images but also look at other images that show the same spot and see how it changed over a period of time.
  • He likes the idea of having a "time capsule" collection of New Mexico landscapes.

User Story:

As a user, I am looking for an easy and quick way to share and upload pictures that I take on our hikes and witness the change in New Mexico's landscapes.

Interaction Flow: Sam Hughes

  • a. Sam recognizes the url and clicks on the link.
